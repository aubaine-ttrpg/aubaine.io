# Turn Structure

## What it is

How a fight is ordered, and what a character can do on their turn.

## How it works

On your turn you get one Action, one Movement, and one Bonus Action. When it is not your turn,
you may take one Action in response to what happens.

Combat runs team by team. The team that starts the fight acts first, with all its members, then
hands play to another team of its choice. No team acts again until every other team has acted.
The aggressor opens; an ambush hands the opening to the ambusher.

## At the table

> **GM:** You kick the door in and the room erupts. You started this, so your side goes first,
> all of you, then the guards answer.

## Design Notes

One Action a turn bounds how much a character spends at once, which keeps a single burst from
ending a fight on its own. The off-turn Action keeps the waiting team in the scene rather than
watching. Acting as a whole team rewards coordinating a plan across the side before the enemy
answers.
