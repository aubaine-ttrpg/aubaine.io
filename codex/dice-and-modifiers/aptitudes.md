# Aptitudes

## What it is

Trained modifiers for specific fields of action. Every character has a value for every Aptitude,
from -1 to 10.

## How it works

Every roll adds one Aptitude alongside one Characteristic. You add whatever value the character
has in that Aptitude:

- **0**: untrained, the baseline. Most fields sit here.
- **-1**: worse than untrained, a genuine weakness.
- **1 to 10**: trained, rising to mastery.

## Design Notes

Because every field resolves as one Characteristic plus one Aptitude, a specialist and a novice
roll the same way; the gap between them is the trained value, not a different procedure. Allowing
-1 lets a field be an active liability, not merely an absence of training.
