# Before and After: Removing AI Tells

## Example 1

### Weak

The players will find themselves drawn into a mysterious village filled with secrets and danger.

### Strong

No one in Bellweather draws water after sunset. The well rope comes up wet with blood and white hair.

## Example 2

### Weak

This dynamic encounter challenges players to think creatively and work together.

### Strong

The floor drops one tile each round. The cultist with the key will jump before the bridge falls.

## Example 3

### Weak

The villain is a morally complex antagonist with a tragic past.

### Strong

Irena poisoned the duke to stop a war. She kept the signed invasion order and shows it only if cornered.

## Example 4

### Weak

The ancient ruins are shrouded in mystery and offer many opportunities for exploration.

### Strong

Every door in the ruin opens from the hinge side. The builders expected visitors with backward hands.

## Example 5

### Weak

This magic item adds depth and intrigue to any campaign.

### Strong

The brass eye shows the nearest lie. Each use makes the user's next true statement sound false.
