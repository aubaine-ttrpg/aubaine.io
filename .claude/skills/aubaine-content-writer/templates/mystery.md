# Mystery Template

## Truth

What really happened.

## Victim, Target, or Problem

Who or what was harmed.

## Culprit or Cause

Who or what caused it.

## Motive

Why it happened.

## Method

How it happened.

## Timeline

Before, during, and after.

## Required Conclusions

For each required conclusion, provide at least three clues.

| Conclusion | Clue A | Clue B | Clue C |
|---|---|---|---|

## Suspects or False Leads

Each needs a reason to look guilty and a truth that can be found.

## Active Pressure

What changes while the characters investigate.

## Complications

Bad theories, frightened witnesses, tampered evidence, faction pressure, deadlines.

## Resolution Paths

How the mystery can be solved, partially solved, misdirected, or exploited.
