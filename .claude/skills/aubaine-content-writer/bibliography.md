## How These Sources Inform the Skill

- The Alexandrian informs robust clue structures, node-based scenarios, and open-table/sandbox resilience.
- GUMSHOE informs the rule that core clues should be reachable and that mystery play is about interpretation.
- Sly Flourish informs strong starts, character review, and portable secrets/clues.
- Dungeon World informs agenda/principles/moves and consequence-driven GM procedure.
- Blades in the Dark informs position, effect, consequences, and progress clocks.
- OSR sources inform player skill, rulings, exploration procedure, reaction, morale, and telegraphed danger.
- Safety sources inform session zero, lines and veils, X-Card-style editing, and consent checklists.
- Accessibility sources inform layout, scanability, screen-reader awareness, spacing, color-independent labels, and plain language.
- System references inform license-aware and math-aware compatibility guidance.
