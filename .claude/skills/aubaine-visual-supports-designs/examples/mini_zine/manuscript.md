## How to use this booklet

Read the premise, choose a hook, then use the keyed locations and escalation table during play.

## Premise

A bronze bell rings under the marsh at midnight. Each toll trades one village memory for one drowned secret.

## Hooks

| d6 | Hook |
|---:|---|
| 1 | A child returns home speaking with the voice of a dead tax collector. |
| 2 | The ferryman refuses to cross after hearing his own funeral bell. |
| 3 | Three houses wake with wet footprints on their ceilings. |

## Core procedure

When the bell tolls, the GM advances the flood clock by 1 and asks one player what memory their character loses. If the group silences the bell before the clock reaches 6, the marsh gives back one secret with a cost.
